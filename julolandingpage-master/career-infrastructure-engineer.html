Infrastructure Engineer (DevOps)

We are looking for highly motivated Senior Android Developer to work closely with it's current development team.


Roles and Responsibilities

  • Ensure manual steps during deployments and migrations and scaling our infrastructure to handle usage growth are minimized.
  • Use automation to decrease deployment time.
  • Assess and implement security measures in every aspect.
  • Identify, obtain, and analyze various metrics gathered from production environment to drive infrastructure improvement priorities.
  • Maintain operational servers and supporting internal servers.
  • Work closely with other JULO engineers, products, data scientists to implement infrastructure requirements for new features. Work closely with all to manage access control for all services.


You should have

  • Min 3 years of modern open source software tools and infrastructure experience.
  • Highly skilled in managing and optimizing large amount of data in SQL. Hands-on experience working with remote Linux/Unix system and tools.
  • Hands-on experience with local data centers and local and international cloud providers. Experience developing, deploying, and managing containers.
  • Great team player, have excellent work ethics, and have solid technical communication skills (Indonesian and English)
  • Bachelor’s degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or similar advanced degree

Nice to have experience

Developing, testing, debugging, and troubleshooting with some programming language, especially Python.

Experience working in agile development process and applications such as Trello.
